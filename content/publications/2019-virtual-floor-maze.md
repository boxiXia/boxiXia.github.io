# Development of a virtual floor maze test – effects of distal visual cues and correlations with executive function in healthy adults

**Authors:** Dario Martelli, Antonio Prado, Boxi Xia, Joe Verghese, Sunil K. Agrawal

**Publication:** *IEEE Transactions on Neural Systems and Rehabilitation Engineering*, 27(10), 2229–2236 (2019)

## Abstract

This study presents the development of a virtual floor maze test and examines how distal visual cues affect performance and correlate with executive function in healthy adults.
